Bible Verse

Isaiah 41:10 reminds us that we are never truly alone, even in our most difficult moments. God speaks words of comfort and reassurance: “Do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand.” This verse encourages us to trust in God’s presence and power, knowing that He can give us courage when we feel afraid and support when we feel weak. It’s a reminder that no matter the challenges we face, we have a source of strength that never fails, helping us move forward with confidence and hope throughout our day.
